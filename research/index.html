Current Work

Classification of Breathing Patterns using Machine Learning Techniques, supervised by Dr. Irena Koprinska.

We explored the possibility of diagnosing respiratory disease by classifying fluctuations in breath intervals and airway mechanics. This method offers and advantage over current diagnosis through spirometry, which is typically difficult to perform and is highly dependent on patient cooperation and effort.
